What is PlayerToo?

PlayerToo is a social media platform that helps people to unify around events and activities. Registered users can do the following:

  • Advertise events to the general public, or to friends only
  • Make friends
  • RSVP to events or un-RSVP
  • Plan/discuss an event with attendees, in its very own chat room
  • Search for events by name, description, category, location, friends-only, and whether you've already created or joined it
  • Edit or delete events that you've created
  • Edit your profile. Make sure to include a picture

Use Cases

PlayerToo was conceived as an app for recruiting people to play games, be it pickup basketball or online video games. But why stop there? I realized that PlayerToo could work just as well to recruit people for almost any purpose. For example: labor-intensive volunteer projects, coversations on specific topics, and a business's promotional events.
